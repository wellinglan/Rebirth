# Journal History Manual Test

1. Start the app and open Journal.
2. Fill at least one reflection question and save.
3. Confirm today's entry appears in Recent Reflections.
4. Open the history item and confirm the read-only detail shows the saved answers.
5. Close the app completely.
6. Restart the app and return to Journal.
7. Confirm today's form is restored and the history item still exists.
8. Change today's reflection and save again.
9. Confirm the history list still contains one item for today.
10. Open the detail again and confirm it shows the updated content.
